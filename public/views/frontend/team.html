Meet the Team

© Riot Esports

Eventvods consists of a team full of enthusiastic people. Eventvods is an online project that started back in 2012 and has grown to be the number 1 source for all esports related videos on demand.

LoLeventVoDs was created on the first of September 2012, shortly before the beginning of the Season 2 World Championships A bunch of dedicated volunteers on Reddit with the goal of providing a spoiler-free, competitive League of Legends viewing experience. This “from the community, for the community” project has steadily grown to serve over 200,000 unique visitors monthly.

Clam-

General Manager

The big boss. Clam has been around the longest, by far. He took over once the original founders of /r/loleventvods stopped updating. Clam updates across all titles we cover, both on the website and on Reddit and takes care of moderation, finance, finding new volunteers and a whole lot more.

Simon

Lead Developer

Simon is the lead developer of the Eventvods website. Contributing to Eventvods since June 2013 he is, among other things, responsible for the spoiler-free code on the subreddits and creating the Chrome and Firefox extensions. Simon was once almost stabbed to death on a date.
